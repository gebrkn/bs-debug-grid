bs-debug-grid

bs-debug-grid is a debugging grid for Bootstrap 3 and 4 layouts.

Press F2 or click to show.

Usage

Embed it on your page:

<script src="__URL__"></script>

or drag this bookmarklet to your toolbar: bs-debug-grid

When the grid is on, click ≡ for additional tools.
